Super Mario 64 Moves

To triple jump, run in the direction you wanna go and press k similarly to jump three times.

To Long jump, run a little bit and Press space and k to do a long jump

To wall kick, or wall jump, run straight into a wall, before you hit the wall, jump and when you reach contact with the wall press jump to preform a wall kick

To side somersault, run a bit, then go the opposite way and Mario will preform a U-Turn. While he's doing that, press k to jump to do a side flip

To Backward somersault, press space, then press k to do a back flip.

To dive, run a little bit, and press j to dive, you can also dive in the air while doing a moving jump

That's all for now, so have fun plaing Super Mario 64

Wing Cap

To find the Wing Cap, collect at least 10 stars in order for a beam of light to show. Once you do that, stand under the beam of light and press I or W to go in look view. Use WASD or the arrow keys to move Mario's head in the yellow patch on the roof. Once you do that, it should take you to the Wing Cap Room.

Once you press the button, all the places that uses Wing Cap should be soild.

Vanish Cap

To get the Vanish Cap, go to ethier the right, or the left door to go to the basement. If it's lock, then you might want to fight Bowser in Bowser in the Dark World to get the key. If you already have the key then go to the door. Next, take a right turn not towards the star door but the other door that leads you to outside. Swim though the water until you see two stone pillars. Use Ground Pound on the two stone pillars, that way the water will drain out. Go though the door, turn to the right and run until you see a hole Go into the hole, and it should take you the Vanish Cap Room

Once you press the button, all the places that uses Vanish Cap should be soild.

Metal Cap

To get Metal Cap, you also going though the basement but this time, your going to Hazy Maze Cave, which is the star door. Go into the black and the game should say "Swimmimg Beast in the Carvan." Once you enter there, go to the right, make a long jump ahead, and go though the door. When you get there, long jump to a platform with a heart, and then long jump to the other side. Just watch out for the rollong rocks. Follow the arrows until you get to a door that you can open. Open it, and it should take you to the elevator that leads to downstairs. Ride the elevator, and make a long jump to the water. Swim though the water until you see Dorrie, the blue sea monster. You can hop on his back and ride on him too :) To get to a higher level, ground pound on Dorrie's back and she'll put her head down. Make sure you get to ride on her head, otherwise she'll put her head back up again. Norrmally, you will get the star thats on the middle of the cave, but your going to go to the door with the red frames. If you made it, go though all the doors until you see another black hole. Go in it, and it should take you to the Metal Cap Room.

Once you press the button, all the places that uses Metal Cap should be soild.
